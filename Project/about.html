About AYBU Board Game Club

Learn more about our club, our founding members, and our mission to bring board game enthusiasts together.

Our Mission & Vision

Mission

Our mission is to create a vibrant community of board game enthusiasts at AYBU, fostering social connections, strategic thinking, and fun through regular gaming events and activities.

Vision

We envision becoming the premier board game community at AYBU, with a diverse collection of games and an inclusive environment where students can learn new games, make friends, and develop valuable skills through play.

Frequently Asked Questions

How can I join the club?

You can join by filling out the membership form on our website and joining our WhatsApp group.

Do I need to bring my own games?

No, the club has a collection of games, but you're welcome to bring your favorites to share!

How often do you meet?

We typically hold events twice a month, with additional special events throughout the semester.
